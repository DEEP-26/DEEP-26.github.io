SASYAM

UNITED FOR SOLUTION

OBJECTIVES

Conduct training programs for farmers, providing hands-on experience in sustainable agricultural techniques.

Supplement farmer income by facilitating multiple agricultural enterprises such as mushroom cultivation, composting, ornamental plant production, and value-added processing and support market linkages by assisting farmers in marketing their produce effectively

Raise awareness about sustainable agriculture practices to enhance soil health, reduce chemical dependency, and improve farm productivity.

Provide internships to students to support their learning in social work.

Develop clear and accessible educational content to ensure easy adoption of sustainable farming techniques.

Our Founder
Mr. Dhruv Sachdeva

"The future of our planet lies in the hands that till its soil." Sasyam Society was founded to address the pressing challenges in Indian agriculture—declining soil health, rising input costs, climate change, and loss of traditional farming wisdom. As an agriculture student and son of a farmer, I started Sasyam to make farming climate-resilient and economically viable. Our mission: restore soil vitality, reduce chemical use, and empower farmers with eco-friendly, science-backed practices. What makes Sasyam unique is its youth-led force—students and professionals from agriculture and veterinary fields working with farmers, not just for them. Sasyam is more than a society—it's a movement to revive farming dignity and rural livelihoods through empathy, knowledge, and youth action
