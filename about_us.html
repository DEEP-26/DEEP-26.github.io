'SASYAM' is not just an NGO initiative, but a stepping milestone to attain our vision of healthy soil health and holistic approach on farming extension. Rejoicing the trust of thousands of farmers residing in the untouched rural corners, our team is solely dedicated to work for farmer welfare and promotion of conservative agriculture education amongst youth to sustain the feeding bowl: which is the key ingredient of our national treasure.

OUR STORY

Random Curiosity

Starting with a minimal curious question, 'Soil is the base support for crops, but what if farmers are completely unaware of this overlooked factor?

Our FOUNDER

It was the point when Mr. Dhruv Sachdeva, CEO Sasyam started dwelling upon the current status of knowledge about soil health among farmers.

Initiation

On receiving shocking results based on his survey where merely 3 out of every 10 farmers were aware of soil health, it lead to upbringing a new initiative 'SASYAM' to tackle the real life on field problems.

Formation

Starting as a small reflection of concern, through our continuous efforts and work ethics we are now connected to several rural agriculturists forming a bond of trust and reliance.


Our Founder
Mr. Dhruv Sachdeva

"The future of our planet lies in the hands that till its soil." Sasyam Society was founded to address the pressing challenges in Indian agriculture—declining soil health, rising input costs, climate change, and loss of traditional farming wisdom. As an agriculture student and son of a farmer, I started Sasyam to make farming climate-resilient and economically viable. Our mission: restore soil vitality, reduce chemical use, and empower farmers with eco-friendly, science-backed practices. What makes Sasyam unique is its youth-led force—students and professionals from agriculture and veterinary fields working with farmers, not just for them. Sasyam is more than a society—it's a movement to revive farming dignity and rural livelihoods through empathy, knowledge, and youth action
