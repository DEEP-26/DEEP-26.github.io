Our Founder
Mr. Dhruv Sachdeva

"The future of our planet lies in the hands that till its soil." Sasyam Society was born out of a deep concern for the challenges plaguing Indian agriculture—declining soil health, soaring input costs, climate unpredictability, poor market access, and the fading knowledge of organic and natural farming practices. Farmers, the very backbone of our nation, are burdened by unsustainable practices and a system that often fails to support them. As a student of agriculture and a son of the soil, I founded Sasyam to bring a change—by making farming both *climate-resilient and economically rewarding*. Our mission is to restore soil vitality, reduce chemical dependency, and empower farmers with practical, eco-friendly solutions rooted in science and tradition. What sets Sasyam apart is its *youth-driven energy. A passionate team of students and young professionals from agriculture and veterinary sciences have come together to contribute their expertise to ground-level impact. Together, we work not just *for farmers—but with them. Sasyam is more than a society; it's a movement to reclaim dignity in farming and regenerate rural livelihoods. We believe change is possible—when knowledge meets empathy, and youth rise for the soil that feeds us all.
